# Payment Skill

Purpose:
Handle payment and bill calculation for rentals.

Responsibilities:
- Calculate total rent
- Generate rental bill
- Prepare invoice details
- Support bill download and WhatsApp sharing

Formula:
Total Rent =
Quantity × Price Per Day × Number Of Days

Rules:
- Quantity must be valid
- Rental days must be valid
- Total amount cannot be negative

Workflow:
1. Get rental details
2. Calculate total amount
3. Generate bill
4. Prepare invoice data

Invoice Includes:
- Customer Name
- Material Name
- Quantity
- Rental Date
- Return Date
- Total Amount
